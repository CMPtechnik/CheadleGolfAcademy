Your privacy matters to us

Cheadle Junior Golf Academy is committed to protecting the personal data of our junior members and their families. This notice explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we store it, and your rights under UK data protection law (UK GDPR).

🏌️ Who we are

Data Controller: Cheadle Junior Golf Academy, operating within Cheadle Golf Club.

Contact: Craig - Junior Organiser / Tom - Professional

We are responsible for deciding how and why personal data about you is collected and used.

📋 What data we collect

We only collect data that is necessary to run our junior competitions and communicate with members and parents. The data we collect includes:

Data Why we collect it Source
Junior's name To identify the player and record competition entries Competition sign-up form
Parent / guardian name To know who is responsible for the junior Competition sign-up form
Email address To confirm sign-ups and send competition updates Competition sign-up form
Phone number For urgent contact on competition days (optional) Competition sign-up form
Competition scores & results To maintain league tables and track member progress Recorded by coaching staff
Handicap information To calculate fair competition results Cheadle Golf Club records

⚖️ Our legal basis for processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests — administering junior golf competitions and communicating with members is a core part of running the junior programme.
  • Consent — where you have provided information voluntarily via our sign-up form, we treat this as consent to use that data for the purposes stated.

🗄️ How we store your data

All sign-up data is stored securely in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Cheadle Golf Club admin staff (Craig). League results and scores are stored in the same Google Sheets environment.

  • Access is restricted to authorised staff only via password-protected login
  • Data is held on Google's servers, which are covered by Google's own security and compliance standards
  • We do not use any third-party marketing platforms or share data with advertisers
  • This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies

🤝 Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Cheadle Golf Club staff — where necessary to organise competitions or tee times
  • Junior Sixes League organisers — team names and scores only, no personal contact details
  • Legal obligation — if required by law or a safeguarding concern arises

🗓️ How long we keep your data

We retain personal data for the duration of the current competition season. Sign-up records and contact details are reviewed at the end of each season and deleted if no longer required.

Competition results and league tables are retained as part of the club's historical records but contain no personal contact information.

✅ Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or your child
  • Correction — ask us to correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Objection — object to us processing your data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact Craig or Tom in the pro shop.

🛡️ Safeguarding

The welfare of junior members is our priority. Any personal data relating to children is handled with extra care in line with England Golf's safeguarding guidelines. Only authorised staff have access to junior member data.

📣 Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please speak to us first and we will do our best to resolve it. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):
